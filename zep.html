About

THE LONG
GAME — always has been.

Zep has been making music for years — long before it was easy to be heard, long before streaming made everyone an overnight act. He's a rapper from Riga, Latvia who put in the reps when nobody was watching.

His style is sharp, cinematic, and deliberate — lyricism that earns its weight, beats that hit with intention. There's a reason his catalog keeps growing: because he never stopped.

"He doesn't chase the wave — he's been making it since before the wave had a name."

From the streets of Riga to wherever music travels, Zep is one of the Baltic's most authentic independent voices in hip-hop today.
